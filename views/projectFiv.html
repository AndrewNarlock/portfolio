UW Online Newsletter Redesign

This was my first design project while attending the University of Washington. The goal of the project was to reorganize and redesign the UWT IAS online newsletter while implementing user centered design startegies that were discussed during the course. This project gave me the opportunity to work on site maps, wireframes, prototypes, user-personas and user research strategies. The final design was built using WordPress and greatly improved the navigability and responsiveness of the online newsletter. The newly implemented ux content strategies were also a major improvement to the website.
