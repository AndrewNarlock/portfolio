About

My name is Andrew Narlock and I graduated from the University of Washington with a Bachelors in Communications. I have also completed The Web Developer Bootcamp 2022 by Colt Steele, hosted on Udemy, which has exposed me to more full stack developemt strategies. My studies focus on User Centered Design strategies and web developement. I am highly interested in furthering my understanding of web design and user experience research and I am ultimately interested in designing and developing different software and games. The projects section hosts a list of projects that I have been able to participate in during my studies along with some personal web design projects.
